The Labyrinth


TRUST - 1

You ignore the suspicious voice and brace yourself. Luckily for you, the fall is short, and you're caught by many philodendron gloriosum leaves.

Hey! Why did you ignore me?

"I want to protect myself while falling. It's common sense," you grumble. You look up and the hole in the dirt you expect to see is replaced with a great blue sky. You're absolutely shocked!

Wow! I'm absolutely shocked!

You make a face at the voice's awful joke, but it melts away when you scan your surroundings. Tall hedges seem to have just appeared. Just where are you...?

I have no clue. Maybe you should start exploring, Shaquila.

You ignore the voice's comment, but rather uncoolly follow its suggestion. After a couple of turns, it's confirmed that you're in a hedge maze. With the forest vegetation that shielded you back in the forest gone, the sun's heat beats down on you. "It's so hot... and I can already feel the sunburn," you sigh.

"Goodday! Or should I say goodnoon? Or even night? Or morning? What is time anyway!"

You look towards where the voice comes form, and spot a tropically green parrot. Your jaw drops! A talking parrot?!?

Caw! You seem hot. I know of a fountain 10 paces South of South from where you face.

"South of South? Isn't that just North?"

Hahahah! What a clever cookie you are! Follow me!

While you don't exactly trust this parrot, your goal isn't to keep Shaquila safe. Then again, this might bring Shaquila further in her journey to stopping the drought. What will you choose to do?
